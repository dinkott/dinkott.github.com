Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Donzo application ("Application") developed by Dinko Tenev ("Service Provider"). The Application is provided as a free service and is intended for use "AS IS".


Information Collection and Use

The Service Provider does not collect, store, or transmit users' personal information through the Application.

Chat messages created and exchanged through the Application are stored locally on users' devices and are used solely to provide the messaging functionality of the Application.

The Application does not collect or store users' geographic location, contacts, photos, browsing history, or other personal content.


Location Permissions and Bluetooth Functionality

The Application does not collect, store, process, transmit, or share precise or approximate location information.

The Application requests Bluetooth and, on certain Android versions, location-related permissions because the Android operating system requires these permissions for Bluetooth scanning, nearby device discovery, and device connectivity features.

These permissions are used solely to enable Bluetooth functionality. The Application does not access GPS coordinates, location history, or geographic location information for tracking, analytics, advertising, or profiling purposes.

Any location-related information remains on the user's device and is not collected by the Service Provider.
